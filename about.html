About AGMV

AGMV is a market access platform built to help product brands achieve predictable business development through curated access, immersive engagements, and multi-city ecosystems.

Why AGMV Exists

Business development in the built environment has always been fragmented, unpredictable, and dependent on personal networks. Product brands struggle to reach the right developers, architects, designers, school operators, and hospitality leaders  even with strong marketing and event investments.

The real challenge isn’t visibility.
The real challenge is access.

AGMV was created to solve this by transforming BD from a chance-based effort into a structured, predictable, multi-city access engine.

We exist to engineer access that drives outcomes.
Key Principles:

Curated, not mass-based

Private, not public

Strategic, not transactional

Systematic, not ad-hoc

Multi-city, not local

Measurable, not subjective
